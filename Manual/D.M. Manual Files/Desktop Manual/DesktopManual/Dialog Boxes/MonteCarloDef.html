Monte Carlo Setup Dialog Box

Clicking on and the other crystal check boxes dictate to Desktop, how to calculate sample density. When one or more crystals are checked density is an average of the crystals weighted by the relative intensity option as defined in the Precipitate Orientation Dialog Box.
