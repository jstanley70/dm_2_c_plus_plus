User Defined Pole Figure

PATH...{Stereographic Projection Object}:Pole Figures:Plot Pole Figure:User Defined...

Plotting Pole figures can help give a deeper understanding of the relationship between outside physical forces and the alignment of crystals, due either to effects on growth or on the deformation of the crystal grains. Desktop provides a number of standard pole figures, however there may be cases when the provided pole figures are inadequate to explore the true experiment. For these situations, you may wish to use the User Defined Pole Figure Dialog Box.
